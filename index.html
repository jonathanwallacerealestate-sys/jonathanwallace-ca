Meet Jonathan

I'm Jonathan Wallace, a full-time REALTOR® with Faris Team Real Estate Brokerage and one of the leading agents working in Simcoe County and Georgian Bay. I don't just work here. I live here, and I've invested in every community I serve, from Tiny and Midland to Penetanguishene and Tay.

I understand this area and the lifestyle it offers, so I can paint a real picture of what it's like to live here. For sellers, that means marketing your home around the life it comes with, the water, the towns, the schools and the shoreline, so it stands out and sells for more. For buyers, it means helping you explore the real value of moving into the area, not just the house. I'm young enough to market a home the way buyers search today, and seasoned enough to price, position and negotiate to the result you're after.
