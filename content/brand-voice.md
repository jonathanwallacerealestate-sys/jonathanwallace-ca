# Brand voice

Jonathan Wallace, full-time Georgian Bay REALTOR with the Faris Team, based in Midland,
Ontario. Read this before writing anything for the site, social, or email. The Make
repurposing automation reads this file too, so captions and posts stay on voice.

## Hard rules
- Never use em dashes. Use commas, periods, colons or parentheses instead.
- Always write the name in full as Jonathan Wallace. Never Jon or John.
- Sensory, concrete copy. No AI filler. Do not use: nestled, boasts, luxurious, won't last.
- Top 5 Reasons format is a numbered list with no bold subheadings.
- Match tone to platform: warm and local for clients, tighter for video scripts.

## Voice
Honest, local, direct. Show, do not boast. Reach for specific Georgian Bay detail
(the water, the towns, the schools, the shoreline) over generic real estate language.
