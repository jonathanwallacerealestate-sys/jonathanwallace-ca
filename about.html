My story

Local roots, a real approach.

I am Jonathan Wallace, a full-time Georgian Bay REALTOR with the Faris Team, and I live and work right here in Midland. Georgian Bay is not a territory I cover. It is home. I know the water, the towns, the schools and the shoreline, and that local read shows up in how I price a home and how I sell it.

I started filming honest home tours on YouTube for a simple reason: buyers and sellers deserve a real look at a home and a neighbourhood, not glossy guesswork. So I walk through Georgian Bay listings on camera and show what a place is actually like to live in. That straight, do-the-work approach is the same one I bring to every sale, whether it is a first home in Penetanguishene or a waterfront property in Tay.

The work has been recognized. I was named Best Realtor in the 2025 Midland Readers' Choice Awards, and I am the number one rated agent in Midland on RateMyAgent with a 4.95 star average. Outside of real estate you will usually find me on the Bay with a fishing rod or my dog, or giving back through Bowl for Kids' Sake with Big Brothers Big Sisters of North Simcoe.
